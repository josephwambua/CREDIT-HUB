Debt counselling:

We offer credit counseling to individuals and businesses that are struggling with debt. Our aim is to help the debtor manage their finances more effectively, reduce their debt, and create a plan to pay off what they owe.

Debt Mediation:

As debt mediators we act as neutral third parties to help individuals or businesses and their creditors negotiate a mutually acceptable agreement to manage and repay outstanding debts. Our goal is to avoid legal proceedings, such as bankruptcy, and to create a workable solution that satisfies both the debtor and the creditors.

Credit Risk analysis for corporates:

We help lenders in evaluating the corporate borrowers credit worthiness and ability to meet their obligations hence avoid the risk of default

Skip Tracing:

As The Credit Hub Africa Consulting we will help you contact that debtor who have defaulted on their payments and cannot be contacted through conventional means to ensure your Debt is paid.

Private Investigation:

We collaborator with private investigator (PI) to locate debtors, gather information about their assets, or uncover other relevant details to aid in the recovery of outstanding debts more so where debtors are difficult to find, have hidden assets, or are otherwise avoiding repayment.

Sovereign Debt Advisory:

As Credit Hub Africa – Consulting , we are keen on and concerned of economic stability for African Countries and their long-term financial health.
We offer specialized advisory services to governments (sovereign entities) regarding their debt management strategies, debt restructuring, or the issuance of new debt to help the sovereign states manage their national debt effectively, ensuring long-term financial stability and sustainability.
