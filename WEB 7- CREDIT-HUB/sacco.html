Sacco Hub

The Savings and Credit Cooperative (SACCO) sector plays a crucial role in the socio- economic development of many countries, particularly in emerging and developing economies. SACCOs are member-owned financial cooperatives that provide savings, credit, and other financial services to their members. The presence of the SACCOS in any economy is imperative .This is better understood through below aspects:

✓ Financial inclusivity by offering financial services the unbanked and marginalized communities

✓ Promotion of Savings Culture

✓ Offering Low-Interest Loans with flexible terms

✓ Economic Empowerment trough support to SMEs & Agricultural development.

✓ Community Development through Sacco Ownership& Social Capital

✓ Capacity Building through Financial literacy and education.

✓ Contribution to National economy through capital formation and employment creation.

Challenges Facing the Saccos

❖ Governance issues,

❖ Limited access to capital,

❖ Regulatory hurdles,

❖ Competition from formal financial institutions.
